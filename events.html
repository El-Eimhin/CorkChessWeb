Cork Chess Club would like to extend its heartfelt condolences to the Flannery family in light of the recent passing of David Flannery

May he rest in peace

August 20th Rapid Tournament

Are you ready to sharpen your wits and immerse yourself in an exhilarating chess experience? Look no further! We are thrilled to invite chess enthusiasts of all levels to the Cork August Rapid Play Tournament, taking place on Sunday, 20th of August. Get your boards ready and mark your calendars for an action-packed day of thrilling chess battles!

Event Details:

  • Date: Sunday, 20th of August
  • Entry Fee: €10
  • The top player will be crowned with €60, while the second and third places will receive €40 and €30, respectively. Additionally, we have exciting grading prizes worth €20 each for four participants.
  • Time Control: 10 minutes plus a 3-second increment per move

Tournament Schedule:

  • 2:00 PM: Registration
  • 2:30 PM: Round 1
  • 3:00 PM: Round 2
  • 3:30 PM: Round 3
  • 4:00 PM: Round 4
  • 4:30 PM: Round 5
  • 5:00 PM: Round 6
  • 5:30 PM: Round 7
  • 6:00 PM: Prize Giving

Venue

36 St Patrick's Quay, Victorian Quarter, Cork, T23 EP20

To secure your spot in the Cork August Rapid Play Tournament, simply register at the venue on the day of the event. Limited slots are available, so arrive early to avoid disappointment. See you on Sunday, 20th of August, for a chess extravaganza like no other!

Club Championship

Every week, Cork Chess Club members gather to compete against each other in a thrilling battle of wits and strategy. This exclusive event provides a fantastic opportunity for our members to test their skills, showcase their talent, and engage in friendly yet fierce competition. Whether you're a seasoned player or a newcomer to the chess scene, the Club Championship welcomes participants of all levels. Throughout the tournament, members face off against each other in a series of intense matches, with each game serving as a stepping stone towards the ultimate goal of becoming the Club Champion. The competitive atmosphere is palpable as players employ their best strategies and tactics, seeking victory and chess mastery. Participating in the Club Championship not only hones your chess skills but also fosters a sense of community and camaraderie among members. It's a wonderful opportunity to meet fellow chess enthusiasts, forge lasting friendships, and share your love for the game.
