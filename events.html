Upcoming Events

31st Cork Congress

We are thrilled to announce the highly anticipated 31st Cork Congress Chess Event, hosted at the prestigious Wollen Mills Hotel in Blarney. The event is taking place from the 6th to the 9th of October. This three-day tournament promises an unforgettable experience for chess enthusiasts of all levels. Immerse yourself in the competitive spirit as players from near and far come together to showcase their skills and vie for the top honors. With various sections catering to different skill levels, from novice to expert, there's a place for everyone to participate and enjoy the thrill of strategic battles on the chessboard. The Wollen Mills Hotel provides a perfect backdrop for this prestigious event, offering comfortable accommodations and excellent facilities. Join us for the 31st Cork Congress Chess Event and be part of an unforgettable chess celebration that combines fierce competition, camaraderie, and the joy of the game. Club Championship

Every week, Cork Chess Club members gather to compete against each other in a thrilling battle of wits and strategy. This exclusive event provides a fantastic opportunity for our members to test their skills, showcase their talent, and engage in friendly yet fierce competition. Whether you're a seasoned player or a newcomer to the chess scene, the Club Championship welcomes participants of all levels. Throughout the tournament, members face off against each other in a series of intense matches, with each game serving as a stepping stone towards the ultimate goal of becoming the Club Champion. The competitive atmosphere is palpable as players employ their best strategies and tactics, seeking victory and chess mastery. Participating in the Club Championship not only hones your chess skills but also fosters a sense of community and camaraderie among members. It's a wonderful opportunity to meet fellow chess enthusiasts, forge lasting friendships, and share your love for the game.
